NEST NEWS
LATEST INVESTMENT MERCHANTZ FROM SF VISITS HK
We were very excited to welcome Merchantz, based in San Francisco, into the Nest portfolio of companies and host them for a whirlwind trip to Hong Kong. The founders Amit and Ronit were connected with potential investors, clients and network partners as well as the Nest family of businesses. Good food, fast-paced and lots of laughs - Hong Kong style!  Read More...
LEVEL39 PARTNERSHIP
We've entered into a collaborative agreement with London-based tech accelerator space Level39, Europe's largest accelerator space which specifically caters for fintech startups. Going forward Nest's network of startups will be able to access both Level39's physical space as well as their resources and network. Level39's startups will similarly be able to tap into our resources here at Nest.  Read More...
JACK MA IN HONG KONG
Jack Ma has recently announced a HK$1 billion investment in HK's young entrepreneurs to encourage the youth here to drive change in the form of technology and innovation. Furthermore, he stressed that for the Alibaba Young Entrepreneur Venture Foundation to be successful, it must be run by dedicated private organisation. As the only private incubator in HK, we wholeheartedly agree! Read More...
QUIT THE MATRIX
Ask yourself, 'are you in control of your own destiny?' Knowing the answer to this question is the single most important element achieve true success. If you responded no, it's time to quit your job and leave the Matrix! An Insights piece by our CEO Simon Squibb  Read More...
