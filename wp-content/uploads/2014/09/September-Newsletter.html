NEST PITCH DAY
We received over 75 applications to our pitch day in early September. 10 talented founding teams were invited to pitch to our panel of investors, the quality and the range of ideas were certainly impressive. We are now progressing to the next steps in the investment process with some of the teams and will feature a couple of them on our equity crowdfunding platform, Investable.

PORTFOLIO NEWS

FOODIE STARTUP MOVEMENT
Foodie is giving back to the startup world by launching the Foodie Startup Movement.


They will cast the spotlight on entrepreneurs behind the most exciting food startups arriving in Hong Kong. As an entire ecosystem of its own, food startups have been attracting the attention of investors and foodies all around the world. Click on the image to follow their progress!
