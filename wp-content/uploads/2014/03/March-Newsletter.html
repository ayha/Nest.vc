Neurofeedback Start Up Joins the NEST Portfolio

With one in ten American children being diagnosed with ADD/ADHD, Playto is producing an EEG headset and tablet-compatible game that can help kids to focus and improve brain functioning. The solution offered by Playto is both fun and clinically proven to be successful. Watch this space as we expect great things to come!

Photo: Playto at Pitch Day December 2013


Ivey Business School X NEST Pitch Day


11 Ivey Executive MBA students and alumni were given 10 minutes to convince a panel of investors that they and their businesses are worth time, mentorship and a money investment. In a scene that resembled an intense episode of Shark Tank, all the founders were subjected to an intensive question and answer session by the panel helping many founders refine their ideas. 

Ivey Pitch Day March 2014

Wine and Cheese Evening at NEST


We had the chance to reconnect with entrepreneurs in our network and aim to host more events like this in the near future. This was the perfect opportunity for entrepreneurs to meet the NEST team, and to connect amongst themselves to exchange ideas and experiences. We have already had a pair of founders join up from this evening and are excited by the potential of future events!
